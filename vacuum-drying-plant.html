Vacuum Drying Plant

Our Vacuum Drying Plant is a high-performance industrial drying system designed for the efficient removal of moisture, gases, and volatile contaminants from transformer active parts, windings, cores, insulation materials, and other electrical components. Built for critical drying applications in the power and electrical manufacturing industry, the system ensures superior insulation quality and long-term equipment reliability.

The plant uses deep vacuum technology combined with controlled heating and precise temperature management to achieve rapid and uniform drying of transformer components. By reducing residual moisture content in solid insulation and active parts, the vacuum drying process improves dielectric strength, insulation resistance, and overall transformer performance.

This system is widely used by transformer manufacturers, motor and generator manufacturers, electrical equipment OEMs, and repair service providers for drying components before oil filling, VPI processing, and final assembly.


Features

  • High-efficiency deep vacuum drying system
  • Uniform heating for fast moisture removal
  • Controlled temperature and vacuum cycle operation
  • PLC / HMI based automatic control system
  • Suitable for transformer active part drying
  • Robust chamber construction with thermal insulation
  • Energy-efficient heating system
  • Low maintenance and continuous-duty design
  • Custom chamber sizes and capacities available
  • Compatible with transformer manufacturing and repair applications

Questions? You’re covered

Designed for demanding industrial use, our Vacuum Drying Plant for transformers and electrical equipment offers reliable drying performance, precise process control, and long term durability. It is engineered to meet the needs of transformer OEMs, repair workshops, and power equipment manufacturers requiring consistent and efficient moisture removal.
Moisture in transformer insulation reduces dielectric strength and insulation life. Vacuum drying removes moisture from deep insulation layers and improves transformer reliability.
The plant uses controlled heating and vacuum conditions to evaporate moisture from insulation materials. The moisture is removed through the vacuum system and condensation process.
Vacuum Drying Plants are used in transformer manufacturing plants, repair workshops, and electrical equipment maintenance facilities for insulation drying processes.
