Overview

Leading Engineering Firm offering Solutions for Global Industries.

At Spera Vacuum Equipment Private Limited, we are committed to delivering high-performance vacuum technology solutions for the global transformer, Motors and electrical sectors.

Established in 2014, Spera Vacuum specializes in advanced engineering design and innovative solutions for the vacuum and transformer industry.

Our product portfolio includes Vacuum Drying Plants, Vacuum Pressure Impregnation Plants, Transformer Oil Filtration Systems, Transformer Air Dryer, Online Dry-out System, Transformer Evacuation Systems, Oil Handling Systems, Industrial Heating Ovens and Oil Storage Tanks. Each system is engineered to improve operational efficiency, enhance insulation reliability, and extend the service life of critical equipment.

Headquartered in Pune, India, we combine advanced technology, stringent quality standards, and customer-focused engineering to deliver dependable solutions worldwide
